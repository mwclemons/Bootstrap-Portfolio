About

Michael Clemons is a Senior IT Business Analyst on the Portfolio Management Team at Essilor of America. He is responsible for meeting with business resources to identify requirements for IT projects. In this role, Michael worked on one the largest IT projects ever delivered by Essilor IT in the Bengal Program. Prior to transitioning into IT, Michael was a Senior Financial Analyst supporting the IT department at Essilor. In this role, Michael supported the CIO, IT leadership and the Lab Management System (LMS) leadership by coordinating the budgeting process to ensure budgeting and forecasting deadlines were met.

Before joining Essilor, Michael was a Financial Analyst at SourceCorp in Dallas. While at SourceCorp, Michael completed his MBA at Southern Methodist University while working fulltime.

Prior to SourceCorp, Michael’s first job after completing his undergraduate work at the California Institute of Technology was as a Risk Analyst at Hunt Financial Ventures in Dallas. As a Risk Analyst, Michael analyzed and monitored the risks of multi-strategy, convertible arbitrage, equity long-short, credit arbitrage, natural resources, special situations and statistical arbitrage hedge funds.
